Brief Bio.

Xiangming Cai received the B.Eng. degree in Information Engineering from the Guangdong University of Technology, Guangzhou, China, in 2017.
He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the Department of Information and Communication Engineering, Xiamen University, Xiamen, China.
His research interests include chaos-based digital communications and their applications to wireless communications.
